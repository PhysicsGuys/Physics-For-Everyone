Simple Explanation:

Energy (E) equals mass (m) times the speed of light (c) squared. Mass and energy are the same thing - just in different forms!

This equation means that a tiny bit of mass contains a HUGE amount of energy. If you could convert just 1 gram of matter (about the mass of a paperclip) completely into energy, it would release the same energy as 20,000 tons of TNT!
