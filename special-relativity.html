Simple Explanation:

The faster you move, the slower time goes for you! And nothing can travel faster than the speed of light!

Imagine you're on a spaceship traveling at 90% the speed of light. To you, everything feels normal. But to someone watching from Earth, you're moving in slow motion! Your clock runs slower, you age slower, and even your spaceship looks shorter! This isn't science fiction - it's real physics that we've proven with experiments!

Time Dilation: If you traveled in a spaceship at 99% the speed of light for what feels like 1 year to you, about 7 years would pass on Earth! Your twin would be 6 years older than you when you returned!

Length Contraction: Objects moving really fast appear shorter in the direction they're moving. A 100-meter spaceship traveling at 99% light speed would look only 14 meters long to someone standing still!

Nothing Goes Faster Than Light: No matter how much energy you use, you can never accelerate past the speed of light. Light always travels at the same speed!

GPS satellites have to account for time dilation! Without Einstein's relativity corrections, GPS would be off by several kilometers every day!
